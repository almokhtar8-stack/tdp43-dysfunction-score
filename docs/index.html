Key Findings

🎯 Perfect Classification

ROC = 1.0 in both Random Forest and Elastic Net models. Complete separation of KO vs Rescue samples.

🧱 ECM Dysregulation Validated

22-gene ECM signature emerges as #1 pathway (p.adj = 0.009), confirming recent literature across multiple cell types.

🔬 TARDBP Autoregulation

TARDBP ranked #10 in feature importance, model independently rediscovered known 3'UTR autoregulation.

💉 Therapeutic Targets

MMP inhibitors (ECM), anti-IL-6 biologics (inflammation), PI3K-Akt modulators (survival) identified.
